TakeProfit and StopLoss

For each pattern, we automatically calculate stop-loss and take-profit levels, helping you visualize target outcomes and know when a pattern gets invalidated (if the stop-loss is hit). The stop-loss is placed right where the pattern becomes invalid, while take-profits are derived from Fibonacci levels and the pattern’s size. All these parameters are fully customizable in the indicator settings.

TrailingStop

A trailing stop can help maximize potential profits when used correctly. Our indicator fully supports this feature, allowing you to secure your gains and optimize your trades.

Backtester Tool

Our unique backtesting tool reveals each pattern’s performance in both short and long positions, showing profit percentages up to the first and second take-profit levels, as well as overall win rate. This in-depth view delivers a clear understanding of how each pattern performs on a given chart.

Active Patterns

The Active Patterns table displays all currently active patterns, including their targets, stop-loss levels on the chart, entry points, and other vital details—all in one place.

Customization Alert

Customizable alerts let you go about your day without constantly watching the charts. Whenever a new pattern appears, our indicator automatically sends a notification right to your phone or computer, so you won’t miss a single opportunity.
